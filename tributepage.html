Justin Trudeau

The 23rd Prime Minister of Canada since 2015

A brief Justin Trudeau's lifetime:

  • 1971 - Born
  • 1994 - Bachelor's Degree on Literature at McGill University
  • 1998 - Degree on Education at Univerity of British Columbia
  • 2006 - Chaired the Liberal Party's task force
  • 2007 - Began his campaign for a Parliament seat
  • 2013 - Became the Liberal Party's leader
  • 2015 - Became the Prime Minister of Canada
